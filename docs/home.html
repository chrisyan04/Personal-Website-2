About Me

Hey, you may know me by know. If not, my name is Chris. I am a budding data scientist interested in machine and deep learning, AI, and on the side some web app development. Albeit being am stuck in front of a computer for a large portion of my day, I am an cheerful and outgoing person who enjoys making new friends where ever I am. Such questions produce more and more desire in me to discover forward with our new age of datafication.
